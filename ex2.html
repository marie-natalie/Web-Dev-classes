Why we collaborate

Our health goals require strong support from experts around the world. WHO currently works with 5 hosted partnerships, more than 100 collaborative arrangements and over 800 collaborating centres. These collaborations are the best way to extend the reach of our programmes, learn from the knowledge and expertise of those in the field and support the objectives of national health systems. We believe improving health care is a global effort, and we are proud to work with a network of collaborators in over 80 countries. Together, we can improve the health and well-being of everyone.

Marie Natalie, 2023, DBIT
